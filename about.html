About Us

Pakistan Housing Finance Company Limited (Pak Housing) was incorporated on June 03, 2020 as an unlisted public limited company. Pakistan Housing as a Non-Banking Finance Company (NBFC) is licensed & regulated by Securities & Exchange Commission of Pakistan to provide housing finance particularly to middle and low income groups to make their dream of owning a home come true.

Pakistan Housing offers Home Loan products, particularly for affordable housing. Pakistan Housing’s Home Loan products are Sharia Compliant and are being vetted by Al Hilal Shariah Advisors for Shariah Compliance. The Shariah Council of Al Hilal is headed by Mufti Irshad Ahmad Aijaz Sahab.

PAKISTAN HOUSING FINANCE

Vision

At Pakistan Housing, we facilitate our customers to own homes by providing affordable housing finance products.

PAKISTAN HOUSING FINANCE

Mission

To be a partner of choice in providing the low and middle-income groups in Pakistan with affordable housing loans.

To focus on the affordable housing space and penetrate this large & growing market to build a scalable business model.

To bring Digital Interventions across the Value Chain to improve customers’ experience and bring efficiencies in the operations.

To build value for our Customers, Employees and Investors, in the process of building the company.

PAKISTAN HOUSING FINANCE

Core Value

Pakistan Housing Finance values govern the purpose of the organization
